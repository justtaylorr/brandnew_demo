About

Brand New is a rock/alternative band from Long Island, New York. The band signed with Triple Crown Records in 2001 and released their debut studio album Your Favorite Weapon. Their second album, Deja Entendu, released in 2003 marked a stylistic change for the band.

Members

  • Jesse Lacey - Lead vocals, guitars
  • Vincent Accardi - Guitars, backing vocals
  • Garrett Tierney - Bass guitar
  • Brian Lane - Drums, percussion
  • Derrick Sherman - Guitar, backing vocals, keyboards (2005-2013)
